Medical Waste

Medical waste, a byproduct of healthcare activities, poses significant environmental and health risks if not managed properly. It includes a variety of items like sharps, pharmaceutical waste, and contaminated materials. To mitigate these risks and promote sustainability, several strategies can be implemented. These include proper waste segregation and disposal, minimizing the use of single-use plastics, adopting reusable instruments, promoting waste reduction at the source, and implementing effective recycling programs
